Western Omelette

A wonderful and hearty omelette with all you're daily needs! Sure to fill up up morning or night!

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • Butter
  • 1/3cup of Milk
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Grated Ham
  • Cheese, a mozzarella and havarti mix preferably
  • Bell Peppers, avoid the green ones
  • Mushrooms

Instructions

  1. Add butter to the pan. Add mixed eggs into the pan with your milk.
  2. Once part the bottom of the omelette has somewhat solidified, add the grated cheese and ham.
  3. Once most of the cheese has started to melt add the sliced mushrooms and peppers.
  4. Fold the omelette in half, toast slightly on both sides. And you're ready to plate it!
