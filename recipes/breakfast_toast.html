Breakfast Salmon Toast

This is the go-to! its easy, its fast and filling due to its high protein content

Ingredients

  • Bread, a slice of toast
  • Creme Cheese Spread
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Smoked Salmon

Instructions

  1. Toast your slice to your appropriate crispiness
  2. Spread the creme-cheese onto the toast
  3. Place the smoked salmon pieces on top
  4. Add salt and pepper to your taste
